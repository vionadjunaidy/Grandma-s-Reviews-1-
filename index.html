Today's Pick

Breakfast

Cafe Batavia, Kota Tua

As a classic-looking cafe, Café Batavia serves over 700 pax per day and has attracted honored and reputable guests from all over the world; Queen of Sweden, Vice President of Argentina, President of Slovakia, Ambassadors of the United States, France & Morocco, and many others.

Lunch

Cork & Screw, Plaza Indonesia

A restaurant and bar inside a wine shop, Cork&Screw is a sophisticated dining destination conveniently located in Jakarta’s hip Thamrin and SCBD area. Our cellar is home to the largest wine selection in the country while the kitchen focuses on an extensive menu comprising of European and Asian favorites.

Dinner

Casa Cuomo Ristorante, Gunawarman

Casa Cuomo Ristorante specializes in Italian-Japanese fusion food, focusing mainly on the pizza and the pastas, which chef Salvatore Cuomo making a branch in Indonesia from the main restaurant in Japan.
