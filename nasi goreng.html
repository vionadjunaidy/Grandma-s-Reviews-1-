Grandma's Nasi Goreng

By Magdalena A.

The joys of childhood, put in a plate of fried rice.

Servings: 4 | Prep: 10 minutes | Cook: 10 minutes

Ingredients

3 tbsp oil to fry the eggs

1 boneless, skinless chicken thigh

100g of frozen mixed vegetables.

1/2 onion, thin sliced shallots, and 1 clove of garlic minced.

1 tbsp of salt. Can be adjusted to preferences.

2 servings of rice, preferable day old.

Green onions for garnish, chop evenly.

2-3 tbsp of sweet soy sauce(kecap manis)

2 large eggs(each around 50g without shell, divided)

Steps

Step 1

Boil the lasagna sheets.

Step 2

Make the sauce.

Step 3

Season with sauce and toppings

Step 4

Bake on the center rack for 40 minutes.

Step 5

Serve and enjoy!
