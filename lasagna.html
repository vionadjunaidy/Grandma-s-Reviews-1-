Lasagna

By John Chandler

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Servings: 12 | Prep: 30 minutes | Cook: 2 hours 30 minutes

Ingredients

12 lasagna sheets

1 pound of ground beef

1 cup of beef broth or water

1 egg, beaten

1 jar of spaghetti sauce

Any cheese, preferable riccotta, mozarella, and Parmesan

Steps

Step 1

Boil the lasagna sheets.

Step 2

Make the sauce.

Step 3

Season with sauce and toppings

Step 4

Bake on the center rack for 40 minutes.

Step 5

Serve and enjoy!
