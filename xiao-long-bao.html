Xiao Long Bao

By TasteMade

Xiao Long Bao, the legendary dim sum that has captivated the world by the sheer impossibility to perfect it.

Servings: 20(Dumplings) | Prep: 1 hour 35 minutes| Cook: 10 minutes

Ingredients

20-25 wrappings, just in case of tearing

150-200ml of chicken stock

30 grams of gelatinI(sheet or powder)

150 grams of ground pork

2 stalks of green onions

3 grams of ginger

5ml of soy sauce

5ml of sesame oil

3 grams of salt and however much pepper to taste

8 grams of sugar

Steps

Step 1

Boil the spaghetti

Step 2

Toast the garlic in olive oil

Step 3

Coat the pasta in the sauce

Step 4

Incorporate the pasta water

Step 5

Serve and enjoy!
